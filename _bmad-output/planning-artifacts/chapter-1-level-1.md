## Learning Objectives

| Objective | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **O1** | Differentiate by ear: Unison, Octave, Fifth, Major third |
| **O2** | Isolate the notes of a major triad (root, third, fifth) |

| **O4** | Memorize and transcribe a 3-note melody |

# Level 1: Discovering Intervals

## Level Objectives

- Discover and recognize: Unison, Octave, Fifth, Major third
- Distinguish melodic interval (successive) and harmonic interval (simultaneous)
- Develop audiation (hearing "in your head")
- Introduction to harmonic resolution (V → I)
